Hi, my name is Zafeirakis Zafeirakopoulos and I am a mathematician spending most of my time designing or implementing algorithms. I hold a Ptychion (4-year degree) in Mathematics and an MSc. in Computational Science from the University of Athens. I was awarded a PhD from the Johannes Kepler University in Linz, under the supervision of Prof. Paule in the Research Institute for Symbolic Computation (RISC). After holding post-doctoral positions at RISC advised by Prof. Peter Paule , at ΕρΓΑ advised by Prof. Ioannis Emiris and at Galatasaray University advised by Prof. Meral Tosun, I am currently an assistant professor at the Institute of Information Technologies at Gebze Technical University.
